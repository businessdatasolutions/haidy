Program Overview

Our AI Strategy Development training program is designed specifically for small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs) looking to harness the power of AI for business transformation. Through a series of interactive workshops, you'll learn to develop and implement effective AI strategies that align with your business goals.

The program consists of three comprehensive workshops, each focusing on a crucial aspect of AI strategy development:

Discover Your AI Strategy

Explore AI's potential impact on your industry, understand strategic implications, and develop a compelling vision aligned with your business goals.

  • ✓ AI Potential & Impact Mapping
  • ✓ Vision Statement Crafting
  • ✓ Leadership Buy-in Strategies

Translate Strategy to Action

Transform your AI vision into concrete business opportunities and use cases, evaluate their potential value and feasibility, and prioritize initiatives.

  • ✓ Use Case Generation
  • ✓ Value vs. Feasibility Analysis
  • ✓ KPI Definition

Implementation Planning

Learn to plan and execute your AI initiatives effectively, manage resources, address challenges, and ensure responsible AI implementation.

  • ✓ Resource Planning
  • ✓ Change Management
  • ✓ Responsible AI Practices
